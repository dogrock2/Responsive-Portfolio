About Me

My name is Alexander Nieves and I enjoy coding. When I was 18 I took a course in electronics in a technical school. I graduated and found a job pretty quick. Around two years later I wanted to learn new skills so I decided to go to school for computer repairs and networking but I only lasted about 2 months because the school went out of business. I then decided to give computer programming a try.


I took a programming course and even though I was able to finish it, I was never able get a job as a programmer. I got discouraged and stopped searching. I did continue to code because I enjoyed it. In the last few years I decided to try again to see if I can get a job in as a programmer. I studied on my own and decided to get a few certifications. I received first the Linux certifications through compTIA and then I went for the Java certifications. I obtained the first two Java programming certifications from Oracle. Finally I decided to go for the Rutgers coding bootcamp to learn up to date technology.
